The studio

Thrown by hand, one piece at a time.

Everything starts on the wheel with local stoneware clay. Each piece is trimmed, dried, glazed by hand and fired twice in our kiln, over the better part of two weeks.

That slowness is why the speckle and the form shift a little from piece to piece. We think a cup you can feel the maker in is worth the wait.
