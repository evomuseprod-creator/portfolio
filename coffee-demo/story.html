Before dawn, while the street is still dark and quiet, the fire is already lit. Small-batch coffee, roasted by hand the night before you ever drink it.

Most coffee is roasted months ago, an ocean away, by no one in particular. Ours is roasted twelve kilos at a time, by one person who listens for the first crack and knows exactly when to pull it.
