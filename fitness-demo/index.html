The work

A method, not a vibe.

01 / Assess

We measure where you actually are.

A 60-minute movement and strength assessment before you lift anything heavy. We find what is tight, what is weak, and what we build first. No guessing, no ego lifts.

02 / Build

Strength blocks, written for you.

You train in blocks with a clear target each week. Squat, hinge, push, pull, carry. The coach loads the bar to your day, not a class average, so progress is real and joints stay happy.

03 / Re-test

Every four weeks, we prove it.

We re-test the same lifts and movements so the line on the page goes up, not just the playlist. You leave knowing exactly what changed and what comes next.

Who coaches you

You get the same coach, every session.

Marcus Tan · Head coach

Marcus spent ten years coaching powerlifters and post-injury clients before opening FORM. He is the one who writes your plan and the one who spots your heavy sets, so nothing gets lost in a handover.

His rule is simple: load the person in front of you, not the program on the wall. Strong for life beats strong for a photo.

Coaching the floor with Marcus: Priya Nair (women's strength, pre and postnatal) and Daniel Foo (Olympic lifting, sport). Eight to ten lifters per session, never more.
