The firm

We keep the practice small on purpose, so the partner you meet is the partner who does the work.

Big firms spread a matter across a team and a budget. We do the opposite. Fewer cases, more attention, and a senior pair of hands on every brief. When the dispute is serious, that is what changes the result.
