Some dinners you eat. This one you sit through, course by course, by the light of the fire.

There is no a la carte and no rushing. Sixteen seats face the open kitchen, one seasonal menu is served, and the evening moves at the pace of the grill. Come hungry and leave the choosing to us.

The chef

Cooked at the counter, by the same hands.

Renee Goh · chef and owner

Renee cooked across wood-fire kitchens in Sydney and Copenhagen before bringing Ember home to Keong Saik. She works the pass every service, so the plate you are handed was finished by the person who wrote the menu.

Her cooking is short on garnish and long on smoke. A few ingredients, treated with respect and a hot fire, served to a room small enough to read.
