The Making

Aged ninety days. Bottled by hand, numbered.

Nº VII is blended in small batches in our atelier, then rested for three months so the smoke settles into the amber and the edges soften.

Only then is it decanted by hand into faceted glass. Every bottle carries its own number, and the maker's mark.
