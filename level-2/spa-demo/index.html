The ritual

Four steps, no hurry.

01

Arrive

Leave the day at the door. A warm tea, robe and slippers, and time to soften before anything begins.

02

Heat

Twenty slow minutes in cedar and steam, until the week leaves your shoulders and your breath drops low.

03

Water

A cold plunge, then warm mineral water. The reset you did not know you needed, twice over.

04

Rest

Wrapped in linen by the window, with herbal tea and nowhere to be. Stay as long as the quiet holds you.
